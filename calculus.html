1. Definitions

Derivative: The rate at which a function changes at any point. Notation: f'(x) or dy/dx.

Integral: The area under a curve of a function. Notation: ∫f(x)dx.

2. Formulas

  • Derivative of xⁿ: d/dx(xⁿ) = n*xⁿ⁻¹
  • Derivative of sin(x): d/dx(sin(x)) = cos(x)
  • Integral of xⁿ: ∫xⁿ dx = xⁿ⁺¹ / (n+1) + C
  • Integral of eˣ: ∫eˣ dx = eˣ + C
